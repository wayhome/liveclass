So you want to build your own data center

作者: dban | 评分: 248 | 评论数: 35 | 发布时间: 2025-01-17 20:41
文章摘要

Railway最初在Google Cloud Platform上构建，但随着发展，GCP引发了一系列问题，威胁到其业务。这些问题包括定价过高（如出口费用）、服务水平受限以及工程限制，阻碍了功能扩展。尽管每年花费数百万美元，获得的支援却极为有限。为此，Railway于2024年1月启动了Railway Metal项目，自行建设物理基础设施。经过五个月的努力，首个服务器在加州上线，并计划扩展至更多数据中心区域。 建设过程中，Railway选择了笼式托管方案，以确保空间、电力和冷却。电力是最大成本，需提前承诺固定月度支出。为保证供电可靠，每个机架配备两个独立电源，并使用可远程控制的电源分配单元（PDU）。 网络方面，Railway选择靠近一级ISP、互联网交换中心和光纤网络的数据中心，以实现低延迟和成本优化。通过这些措施，Railway旨在为客户提供“无基础设施”的云体验。

The National Archives is seeking volunteers to help transcribe and organize millions of historical documents, many written in cursive and difficult to read. Through the Citizen Archivist program, volunteers make these documents more accessible by transcribing them, improving searchability for researchers and history enthusiasts. The agency digitizes tens of millions of records annually but relies on human volunteers for accurate transcriptions due to limitations in artificial intelligence and optical character recognition technology. Over 5,000 volunteers have joined, working on projects like Revolutionary War pension files. The program is open to anyone with an internet connection, offering flexible participation. The initiative also highlights the resurgence of cursive instruction in schools, with over 20 states now requiring it. Volunteers find the work rewarding, connecting with history and uncovering personal stories from the past.
